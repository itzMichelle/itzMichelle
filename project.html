One of the projects I'm most proud of is my 'eating green' project which is a menu targeted for vegans. I came up with my layout by organizing images that could catch the attention of the buyer, vegans, and making the text smaller than the images but still readable. My layout matches my target audience because it includes images of the desired food and it’s ingredients. The images are organized with the recipe so the buyer can see what they’re ordering and possibly catch their attention.
